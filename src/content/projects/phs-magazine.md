As Graphical Design Editor for the Population Health Sciences Magazine, I worked on the part of design that often matters most but is hardest to summarize: judgment. The publication needed a visual system that stayed accessible without flattening the seriousness of the material.

That experience sharpened the editorial side of how I work on web projects now. It trained me to think about pacing, hierarchy, and how visual choices shape trust.
