ScentSense is the clearest example of the kind of product work I want to keep doing. The interface is calm and editorial, but underneath that is a recommendation workflow that has to make coherent decisions from taste signals chosen by the user.

The repository is private, so this case study focuses on the shape of the product rather than internal code. The important thing is the combination: framing, interaction design, data handling, and recommendation output all had to work together to make the site feel convincing.
