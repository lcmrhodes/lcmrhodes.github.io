This project matters because it demonstrates how I think about staged systems: not every case needs the most expensive or complex path, and a good workflow should make that escalation logic visible.

The Shiny app and supporting artifacts were a way of making the cascade intelligible rather than leaving it locked inside scripts and outputs.
